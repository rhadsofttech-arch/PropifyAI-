FAQs

Property Questions Answered.

Get answers to common questions about property search, verification, and transactions on our platform.

Contact Support
How does MyPropifyNG verify property listings?
We employ a rigorous 3-step verification process that includes document authentication, physical inspection where possible, and partner validation. Our team ensures every listing meets our quality standards before going live on the platform.
What are the costs for using MyPropifyNG services?
Property seekers pay zero fees to search, browse, and connect with verified listings on our platform. We offer affordable and scalable plans for property owners and agents looking to list and market their properties.
How long does it take to find a property through your platform?
Our AI matching engine delivers your first property recommendations within 24 hours of completing your profile. Most users find shortlisted properties within 2–7 days depending on their requirements and city.
What support do you provide during property transactions?
We offer 24/7 support throughout your transaction journey — from initial search to key collection. This includes secure messaging, document verification assistance, and escrow payment protection to keep your funds safe.
Which Nigerian cities does MyPropifyNG currently cover?
We currently serve Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt with verified listings. We're expanding to 5 major Nigerian cities by 2026 — including Ibadan, Kano, and Enugu — as our verified partner network grows.
