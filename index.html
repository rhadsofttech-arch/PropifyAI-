Does a Deed of Assignment give me full ownership in Lagos?
Not automatically. A DoA transfers the seller's interest to you, but you still need to obtain Governor's Consent from the Lagos State government within 6 months to perfect the title. Without it, the transaction is not fully enforceable. Consent fees are typically 3–5% of the property value.
