KYC Verification

Why We Verify Every
Property Owner

Nigeria's real estate market is plagued by fraud. That's why every property owner on MyPropifyNG must pass our KYC process before listing a single property.

Government-Issued ID

We verify your identity using an official Nigerian government document to confirm you are who you say you are.

National ID Card (NIN)
International Passport
Driver's License
Permanent Voter's Card

Phone Verification

A valid Nigerian phone number that can be reached and verified confirms active presence and reachability.

Valid Nigerian number (MTN, Glo, Airtel, 9mobile)
OTP confirmation sent to your number
Must match the registered name

Email Verification

A valid email address ensures you receive all platform notifications, payment confirmations, and security alerts.
